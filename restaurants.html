The Top Five Restaurants in Nashville

  • Hattie B's
  • Prince's Hot Chicken Shack
  • Mitchell Deli
  • Two Ten Jack
  • The Treehouse Restaurant



Hattie B's

Hattie B’s Hot Chicken is one of the top restaurants that serves mild to very spicy fried chicken. With portion size much bigger than average, Hattie B’s provides an abundance of food at a reasonable cost. They even serve children’s sizes as well, so the whole family can enjoy.

Prince's Hot Chicken Shack

If you have a loving for hot chicken, you might check out Prince’s Hot Chicken Shack as well. With a more casual vibe, Prince’s Hot Chicken is one of the hottest around. Thorton Prince is said to be the father of Nashville’s hot chicken, although the story has it that it was his lover who actually created the recipe - he just perfected it.

Mitchell Deli

Mitchell Delicatessen specializes in fresh artisan sandwiches. They are proud of their high-quality ingredients and they focus on supporting the local economy. Not only do they serve sandwiches, but they also have a breakfast bar, salad bar, and house made soup.

Two Ten Jack

Two Ten Jack is one of Nashville’s finest pubs, or izakaya. It is Japanese-inspired pub that serves ramen, sushi, and other Japanese-inspired comfort food with a mix of local, Southern ingredients. They have many beers and wines on hand.

The Treehouse Restaurant

The Treehouse Restaurant prides itself on its inclusion of Vegetarian, Pescatarian, Vegan, and Gluten Free foods, allowing for everyone to enjoy their food. They have an abundance of different types of food. However, it is important to make a reservation as they fill up fairly quickly.
